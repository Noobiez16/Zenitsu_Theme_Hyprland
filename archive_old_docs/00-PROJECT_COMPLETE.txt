  Look for @define-color section at the top
  Reload: SUPER + ALT + R

  Reload: SUPER + ALT + R

  Edit: ~/.config/waybar/style/⚡ [Zenitsu] Thunder Glow.css
  Find @keyframes sections
  Adjust timing, colors, or intensity
  Reload: SUPER + ALT + R
